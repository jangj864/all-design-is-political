Spring 24 Design in the Context of Cold War Intro to design

All Design Is Political

Aidana Bergaliyeva Jinhyeok Jang Séa Tanaka

Despite their seemingly distinct paths, capitalist and communist designs influence each other, revealing shared commonalities.
This exhibition explores and compares design ideologies within these two contrasting political contexts.
