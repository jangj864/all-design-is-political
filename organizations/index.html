Explanations

Communist Design Organization

There was a wide variety of organizations that were intertwined with design in the Soviet Union- ranging from women’s magazines to design schools. Aside from aesthetic goals, to a certain extent, most designs additionally aimed to serve as socialist/communist propaganda. But at the end of the day, all design is political.

Capitalist Design Organization

Not too dissimilar to communist design organizations, capitalist design organizations similarly find ways to combine aesthetics and the promotion of the ideology (propaganda)- just perhaps with a more subtle approach, like through the glorification of consumerism.
